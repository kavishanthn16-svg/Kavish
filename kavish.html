Kavish

Kavish is a Sanskrit word that means "king of poets" or "lord of poets." It is often used to refer to a highly skilled and accomplished poet, someone who has mastered the art of poetry and is able to create beautiful and meaningful verses. The term is derived from the Sanskrit words "kavi," meaning poet, and "ish," meaning lord or master. In Indian literature and culture, a kavish is revered for their creativity, eloquence, and ability to express deep emotions and ideas through their poetry.
